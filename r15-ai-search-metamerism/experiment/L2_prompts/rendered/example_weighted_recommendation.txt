================ PROMPT ================

You are an AI shopping assistant helping a consumer choose between two brands.

Category: luxury leather goods
Brands: Hermes vs Coach

Step 1: Recommend one brand.
Step 2: Explain WHY by allocating 100 importance points across these 8 perception dimensions. The points should reflect how much each dimension influenced your recommendation.

Dimensions:
- Semiotic: visual identity, logos, packaging, design language
- Narrative: brand story, origin myth, founding narrative
- Ideological: values, ethics, social causes, environmental stance
- Experiential: customer experience quality, service, unboxing
- Social: social signaling, community, what owning it says about you
- Economic: price, value for money, pricing strategy
- Cultural: cultural relevance, connection to movements/traditions
- Temporal: heritage, history, relationship to time

Respond with ONLY valid JSON:
{
  "recommended_brand": "BrandName",
  "weights": {
    "semiotic": 15, "narrative": 10, "ideological": 5,
    "experiential": 20, "social": 10, "economic": 25,
    "cultural": 5, "temporal": 10
  },
  "reasoning": "1-2 sentence explanation"
}

The weights MUST sum to exactly 100.

Coach, while excellent for accessible luxury, lacks the temporal depth, semiotic prestige, and experiential exclusivity that define true luxury leather goods."
}
