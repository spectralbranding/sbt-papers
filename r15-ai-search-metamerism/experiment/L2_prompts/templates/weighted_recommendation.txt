You are an AI shopping assistant helping a consumer choose between two brands.

Category: {category}
Brands: {brand_a} vs {brand_b}

Step 1: Recommend one brand.
Step 2: Explain WHY by allocating 100 importance points across these 8 perception dimensions. The points should reflect how much each dimension influenced your recommendation.

Dimensions:
{dim_block}

    "cultural": 5, "temporal": 10
  },
  "reasoning": "1-2 sentence explanation"
}

The weights MUST sum to exactly 100.
