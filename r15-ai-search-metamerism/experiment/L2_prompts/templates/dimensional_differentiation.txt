PROMPT TYPE: dimensional_differentiation
PURPOSE: Structured 0-10 difference scoring per dimension. Asks the model to rate how DIFFERENT two brands are on each of the 8 SBT dimensions, plus an overall scalar score.

PARAMETERS:
- {category}: product category
- {brand_a}: first brand name
- {brand_b}: second brand name
- {dim_block}: rendered dimension descriptions

TEMPLATE:

You are a brand analyst comparing two brands for a consumer research report.

Category: {category}
Brands: {brand_a} vs {brand_b}

Rate how DIFFERENT these two brands are on each of 8 perception dimensions.

Dimensions:
{dim_block}

Respond with ONLY valid JSON:
{
  "differentiation": {
    "semiotic": 7.5, "narrative": 8.0, "ideological": 6.0,
    "experiential": 5.5, "social": 8.5, "economic": 9.0,
    "cultural": 7.0, "temporal": 9.5
  },
  "overall_score": 0.78,
  "summary": "1-2 sentence summary of the key differences"
}

Each score: 0.0 (identical) to 10.0 (completely different).
overall_score: 0.0 to 1.0 summary measure.
